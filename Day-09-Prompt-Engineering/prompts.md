# Day 9: Marketing Prompts (Zero-Shot & Few-Shot)

## Understanding Prompting Techniques

**Zero-Shot Prompting:** You ask the AI to perform a task without giving it any examples of what the output should look like. It relies entirely on its pre-existing knowledge.

**Few-Shot Prompting:** You provide the AI with a few examples (usually 2-5) of the desired input and output format before asking it to perform the actual task. This helps the AI understand the style, tone, and specific format you're looking for.

## 1. Social Media Posts

### Zero-Shot Prompt
**Prompt:**
Write a catchy Twitter post to announce our new eco-friendly running shoes. Include relevant hashtags. Target audience is environmentally conscious runners.

### Few-Shot Prompt
**Prompt:**
Here are some examples of our brand's Twitter posts:

Example 1:
Product: Lightweight Windbreaker
Post: Don't let the wind slow you down! 💨 Our new AeroLight Windbreaker is here. Perfect for those chilly morning runs. 🏃‍♂️💨 #AeroLight #RunningGear #MorningRun

Example 2:
Product: Hydration Backpack
Post: Stay hydrated, stay ahead. 💧 The AquaTrek Backpack holds 2L of water and feels like wearing nothing at all. Trail runners, this one's for you! 🌲🏃‍♀️ #TrailRunning #StayHydrated #AquaTrek

## 2. Email Marketing Campaigns

### Zero-Shot Prompt
**Prompt:**
Write a promotional email for a 20% off summer sale on our online bookstore. The email should have an engaging subject line, a friendly greeting, a brief description of the sale (ends this Friday), and a strong call-to-action to visit the website.

### Few-Shot Prompt
**Prompt:**
Write a promotional email for a bookstore based on these examples.

Example 1:
Event: Winter Holiday Book Sale, 15% off
Subject: Cozy up with a good book! ❄️ 15% off all titles
Body: Hi [Name], the weather is frightful, but our books are so delightful! For the next 3 days, enjoy 15% off everything in store. Grab a blanket, a hot cocoa, and your next favorite read. [Shop the Sale]

Example 2:
Event: Back to School Sale, Buy 1 Get 1 50% off
Subject: Get ready for class! 📚 BOGO 50% off textbooks
Body: Hello [Name], summer is winding down and it's time to hit the books. To help you prepare, we're offering Buy 1 Get 1 50% off on all educational materials until Sunday. Start the semester right! [Browse Textbooks]

## 3. Product Descriptions

### Zero-Shot Prompt
**Prompt:**
Write a compelling product description for a smart coffee mug that keeps your coffee at the exact temperature you set via a smartphone app. Keep it under 100 words and highlight the convenience.

### Few-Shot Prompt
**Prompt:**
Write a product description based on these examples.

Example 1:
Product: Noise-Cancelling Headphones
Description: Silence the world and dive into your music. Our premium noise-cancelling headphones deliver crystal-clear audio with deep bass, blocking out distractions whether you're commuting or focusing at work. Enjoy up to 30 hours of wireless playback on a single charge.

Example 2:
Product: Ergonomic Office Chair
Description: Say goodbye to back pain. Our ergonomic office chair provides personalized lumbar support and adjustable armrests to keep you comfortable through your longest workdays. Breathable mesh ensures you stay cool, even under pressure.
