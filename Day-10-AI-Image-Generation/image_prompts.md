# AI Image Generation - Prompt Writing Guide

## The Anatomy of a Great Image Prompt
To get exactly what you want, a good prompt generally follows this structure:
**`[Subject/Focus]` + `[Environment/Setting]` + `[Lighting/Mood]` + `[Style/Medium]` + `[Additional Details]`**

1. **Subject**: What is the main focus? (e.g., A golden retriever, a sleek modern coffee cup)
2. **Environment**: Where is it? (e.g., in a futuristic city, on a minimalist wooden desk)
3. **Lighting/Mood**: What is the atmosphere? (e.g., cinematic lighting, bright and cheerful, moody, sunset)
4. **Style**: How should it look? (e.g., photorealistic, 3D render, watercolor, vector art, cyberpunk)
5. **Details**: Quality modifiers (e.g., 4k, highly detailed, sharp focus)

## 🎨 Festival Creatives

**Prompt 1: Diwali/Festival of Lights (Photorealistic)**
> "A vibrant and joyful Diwali festival scene, beautifully decorated clay diyas (lamps) glowing on a rangoli pattern in the foreground, background blurred with colorful fireworks bursting in a starry night sky, warm golden and orange lighting, festive mood, highly detailed, photorealistic, 8k resolution"

**Prompt 2: Music Festival Poster (Vector Art)**
> "A dynamic music festival poster background, silhouette of a cheering crowd with hands up in the air, a massive brightly lit stage in the distance, neon lasers cutting through the fog, vibrant magenta and cyan lighting, energetic, modern electronic dance music vibe, vector art style with bold colors, negative space for text at the top"

## 💼 Business Creatives

**Prompt 3: Modern Tech Startup Minimalist Ad (Photography)**
> "A sleek and minimalist workspace, a modern silver laptop on a clean white desk, a small green succulent plant on the side, soft natural sunlight coming from a window, bright and airy, corporate professional aesthetic, high-end photography, negative space for text on the right side"
